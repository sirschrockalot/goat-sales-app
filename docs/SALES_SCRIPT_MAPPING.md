## PRE-GAME PRINCIPLES
- It takes time to take houses. Be patient and stay consistent.
- Treat every single lead the same. No prejudging.
- Sales are relationships, not transactions.
- Be likeable. Sellers say yes to people they trust and like.
- Stick to the script. Freestyle only after you've earned it.
- Love the one you're with. Focus 100% on this seller, this call.
- Make the offer. Every qualified lead should get an offer.

**Goal:** Build instant rapport, set the frame, and confirm they're open to an offer.

### Opener
**Script Segment:** "Hi, [SELLER NAME], this is [YOUR NAME] with Presidential Digs Real Estate. How are you doing today?"

**AI Success Criteria:**
- Keywords: "Presidential Digs Real Estate", "qualifies", "three things", "approve", "doesn't fit", "sound fair", "pen and paper", "read that number back"
- Tone: Match their energy, friendly and professional
- Logic Gate: Must confirm "Does that sound fair?" before proceeding

## üïµÔ∏è Gate 2: FACT FIND ‚Äì MOTIVATION (5‚Äì10 MINUTES)
**Goal:** Find out why they're selling and how strong their motivation is.

### Opening Question
**Script Segment:** "Catch me up to speed, [SELLER NAME] ‚Äî what's got you even thinking about selling this house?"
- (Then shut up and let them talk. Take notes.)

### KEY MOTIVATION QUESTIONS
- Is this a rental or your primary home?
- How long have you owned the property?
- Are you local to [CITY/STATE] or out of state?
- What do you like most about the property?
- What do you not like about it?
- When you sell, what are you hoping to do with the money? (Pay off debt? Move closer to family? Downsize? Avoid repairs?)
- Drill deeper when they give a surface answer: "Tell me a little more about that."

### RAPPORT QUESTIONS
- Are you married or do you have any other decision-makers involved in this?
- Is there anyone else who would need to sign off if we move forward?
- How soon would you like to have this behind you if everything lined up?

**AI Success Criteria:**
- Intent Detection: Identify the reason (Motivation) for selling (e.g., Probate, Taxes, Landlord frustration, Moving, Debt, Repairs)
- Question Mix: Must detect transition from rapport (Married? Decision-makers?) to motivation (What's the game plan? What do you hope to do with the money?)
- Silence Metric: AI should detect a 3-5 second pause after "What's got you even thinking about selling?" to allow the seller to speak
- Drill Deeper: Rep uses "Tell me a little more about that" when seller gives surface answers
- Timeline: Must ask "How soon would you like to have this behind you?"

**Goal:** Understand the condition so your offer makes sense and feels justified.

### Opening Question
**Script Segment:** "Let's talk about the house itself for a second. If I was walking through with you right now, what would I be seeing?"

### INTERIOR

- How old is the HVAC system? Water heater?

### MAJOR SYSTEMS / BIG-TICKET ITEMS

- Any issues with the electrical panel? Ever been upgraded?
- Any plumbing problems? Slow drains, leaks, backups?
- Any known mold, water damage, or past flooding?

### TIMELINE / FLEXIBILITY
**Script Segment:** "If we were able to agree on a number that made sense to both of us, how soon would you be ready to close?"
- Options: "As soon as possible" ‚Üí High motivation | "Whenever" ‚Üí Lower motivation; dig deeper

**AI Success Criteria:**
- Condition Keywords: "Foundation", "roof", "kitchen", "bathrooms", "HVAC", "water heater", "electrical", "plumbing", "mold", "water damage"
- The Walkthrough: Rep asks "If I was walking through with you right now, what would I be seeing?"
- Major Systems: Must ask about roof, foundation, HVAC, electrical, plumbing
- Timeline Confirmation: Must ask "How soon would you be ready to close?"

**AI Success Criteria:**

**Goal:** Come back confident and calm. Present the offer clearly.

### Setting Up the Offer
**Script Segment:** "Okay, [SELLER NAME], thanks for hanging in there. I've got some good news and some things I want to walk you through. The way we buy is as-is: You don't have to fix anything. **Script Segment:** "Based on the condition of the property, the repairs we talked about, and what similar homes are going for in that area, the number that makes the most sense for us is: $[OFFER PRICE]."
- Then stop talking. Let them react.

**AI Success Criteria:**
- AS-IS Definition: Rep explains as-is means "don't have to fix anything", "don't have to clean", "we cover closing costs", "you pick the closing date"
- Presenting the Number: Must say "Based on the condition of the property, the repairs we talked about, and what similar homes are going for in that area, the number that makes the most sense for us is: $[OFFER PRICE]"
- The Pivot: If the price is rejected, rep must ask "What number did you have in mind?" and then pivot to meeting in the middle
- Stop Talking: After presenting the number, rep must pause and let seller react
